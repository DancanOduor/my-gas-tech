About Us


MY-GAS-TECH Limited provides the ultimate online plan that links the cooking gas suppliers with their customers via mobile app and GPS technology.

Our aim is to give cooking gas suppliers in Kenya an opportunity to grow their business by providing timely deliveries and excellent services to their customers. With our mobile app, the convenience of customers to receive gas on real time basis is enhanced and with less hustle. Everything in our online gas delivery platform is automated, from placing an order till delivery. MY-TECH-GAS app technology will positively change the way gas delivery business is conducted.

How MY-GAS-TECH Works



  • You can visit our website or simply download our mobile app

  • You can place your order at any time

  • After you order; your order will then be broadcasted to our nearby verified gas delivery agents via GPS

  • Our agents will be able to view and accept your order via MY-GAS-TECH Delivery Agent app.

  • Once your order is accepted we send you a notification

  • You can also schedule a delivery at your convenient time.

Why Use MY-GAS-TECH



  • It enhances the way cooking gas is ordered and delivered by the customers.

  • It Allows you to order your gas at the touch of your mobile screen without calling.

  • It gives the customer an opportunity to choose best gas suppliers, and ones broadcast is done deliveries are instant.

  • We also have information sharing platform by customers and agents through instant alerts of orders.

  • Uses only proffesional agents with physical presence on selected outlets.
